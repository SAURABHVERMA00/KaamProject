Required Attributes*

Data will be needed and should have some fixed attributes and must be as follows and in same order:

  1. DATE_TIME: This column represents the timestamp for each observation.
  2. PLANT_ID: It appears to be a common identifier for the entire dataset.
  3. SOURCE_KEY: This attribute likely serves as a unique identifier for each inverter within the plant.
  4. DC_POWER: This column represents the amount of direct current (DC) power generated by the inverter at each time interval (kW).
  5. AC_POWER: This column represents the amount of alternating current (AC) power generated by the inverter at each time interval (kW).
  6. DAILY_YIELD: It gives an idea of the total energy produced by the inverter during the day.
  7. TOTAL_YIELD: The total yield or energy produced by the inverter since its installation or the beginning of the data collection period.
  8. EXPORTED_POWER: This column represents the distributed power.
  9. LOAD_CONSUMPTION: This column represents the power consumed. It may sometimes exceed the generated power.
